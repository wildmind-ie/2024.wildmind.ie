]]> Family Activities http://wildmind.ie/events/event/family-activities/ Mon, 15 Apr 2024 18:15:17 +0000 http://2024.wildmind.ie/?post_type=event&p=462 Continued]]> Fun and activities for all the family from 11  to 5 on Sunday.

 

School poetry / art

 

Bee mobile

Kids creative workshop suitable for ages 5 and up, presented in conjunction with Tralee Bay Wetlands Centre

 

Make a fire

 

Wildcrafting with Darrach O Murchú
A mini workshop where you will use tools and your imagination to make a wooden pendant, a clay mini beast and hapa-zome leaf bashing. You can take your creations home with you. This is a drop-in workshop that will be located by the information stand, come and go as you please.

 

Tote Bag Screen printing and Lino Printing
Kinch Design Studio will present and supervise a screen-printing station on Sunday afternoon! The family-friendly print station is set up to provide everyone with the ability to print their very own WildMind tote bag. For a more hands on experience why not join their lino printing session.

 
Wise Wool
Join Kerry and Lisa for  a workshop on wet felting using raw wool from the Black Valley. They will share the value of wool as they teach the little ones how to wet felt raw wool together to make a square wall decoration.

This free workshop is suitable for  ages 8+

 

Blue Flag Beats
With Urs Wenk of Drum Dance Ireland
Experience the power of drumming in nature. open to everyone, regardless of age, ability and experience. There will be over 50 drums and percussion instruments but feel free to bring your own (and a folding chair!).

 
Random Acts of puppetry

Archery
Try your hand at shooting some arrows at targets with Ríocht Archery Club. Suitable for adults and children of all ages and all abilities.
Bring a strong arm and a keen eye

]]> Carve and Print with Lino http://wildmind.ie/events/event/carve-and-print-with-lino/ Sun, 14 Apr 2024 21:01:54 +0000 http://wildmind.ie/?post_type=event&p=389 Continued]]> This print-making workshop is hosted by Darragh, of Kinch Design Studio, and will focus on a traditional printing method known as “lino printing”. The workshop will teach beginners how to design, transfer and carve a unique coastal image into lino block. Once the carving process is complete, each participant will take turns printing their very own handmade artwork onto paper. No previous experience is required and all are welcome (16+). All materials needed to complete this workshop will be supplied at the venue (lino block, tools, inks, paper).

Book now to avoid disappointment.
