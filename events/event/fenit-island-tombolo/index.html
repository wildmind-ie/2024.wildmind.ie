Fenit Island Tombolo

Join Fenit Coast Conservation for a walk and talk around the unique Fenit Island Tombolo and learn about their work to preserve the dunes and the local habitats.

This will be a leisurely walk, for about 45 minutes, and includes a demonstration of marram grass planting.

Suitable for all ages.

 

2.00pm Saturday 27th, from the car park at the end of Island Road by the tombolo, or 1.45 pm from the Fenit Coast Conservation stand in the tent. (10 minutes walk).
